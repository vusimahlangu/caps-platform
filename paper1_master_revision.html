Section 1: Algebra & Nature of Roots

Master the Discriminant \(\Delta = b^2 - 4ac\). Drag the slider to find the "Equal Roots" point (\(\Delta = 0\)).

\(x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a}\)
\(\Delta > 0 \Rightarrow\) Real, Unequal
\(\Delta = 0 \Rightarrow\) Real, Equal
\(\Delta < 0 \Rightarrow\) Non-Real

Section 3: Essential Finance Formulas

Growth: \(A = P(1+i)^n\)
Decay: \(A = P(1-i)^n\)
Future Val: \(F = \frac{x[(1+i)^n - 1]}{i}\)
Present Val: \(P = \frac{x[1 - (1+i)^{-n}]}{i}\)
