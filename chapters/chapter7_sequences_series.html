Introduction

Sequences and series deal with patterns in numbers. Many learners struggle because they try to memorise formulas without understanding the pattern first. In this chapter, we identify the pattern slowly before calculating anything.

Basic Definitions

  • Sequence: A list of numbers following a rule
  • Term: Each number in the sequence
  • Series: The sum of the terms of a sequence
  • nᵗʰ term: A formula to find any term

Arithmetic Sequences

An arithmetic sequence has a constant difference between terms.

General form:

Tn = a + (n − 1)d

  • a = first term
  • d = common difference

Worked Example 1 (Very Detailed)

Given the sequence: 5, 8, 11, 14, … Find the 10ᵗʰ term.

Step 1: Identify the first term

a = 5

Step 2: Find the common difference

8 − 5 = 3, so d = 3

Step 3: Write the formula

Tn = 5 + (n − 1)3

Step 4: Substitute n = 10

T10 = 5 + 9×3

Step 5: Calculate

T10 = 5 + 27 = 32

Answer: The 10ᵗʰ term is 32

Arithmetic Series

An arithmetic series is the sum of terms of an arithmetic sequence.

Formula:

Sn = ½n[2a + (n − 1)d]

Worked Example 2

Find the sum of the first 20 terms of the sequence: 3, 7, 11, …

Step 1: Identify values

a = 3

d = 4

n = 20

Step 2: Substitute into formula

S20 = ½(20)[2(3) + 19×4]

Step 3: Simplify

S20 = 10(6 + 76)

Step 4: Calculate

S20 = 10 × 82 = 820

Answer: Sum = 820

Geometric Sequences

A geometric sequence has a constant ratio between terms.

General term:

Tn = arn−1

  • a = first term
  • r = common ratio

Worked Example 3

Given the sequence: 2, 6, 18, … Find the 8ᵗʰ term.

Step 1: Identify values

a = 2

r = 3

Step 2: Substitute

T8 = 2 × 37

Step 3: Calculate

T8 = 2 × 2187 = 4374

Answer: The 8ᵗʰ term is 4374

Geometric Series

A geometric series is the sum of a geometric sequence.

Formula:

Sn = a(1 − rn) ÷ (1 − r)

Worked Example 4

Find the sum of the first 6 terms of: 5 + 10 + 20 + …

Step 1: Identify values

a = 5

r = 2

n = 6

Step 2: Substitute

S6 = 5(1 − 26) ÷ (1 − 2)

Step 3: Calculate

S6 = 5(1 − 64) ÷ (−1)

Step 4: Simplify

S6 = 315

Answer: Sum = 315

Practice Exercises

  1. Find the 15ᵗʰ term of: 4, 9, 14, …
  2. Calculate the sum of the first 12 terms of: 7, 10, 13, …
  3. Find the 6ᵗʰ term of: 3, 6, 12, …
  4. Calculate the sum of the first 5 terms of: 2 + 4 + 8 + …

Summary

  • Arithmetic: constant difference
  • Geometric: constant ratio
  • Identify pattern before formula
  • Use correct formula carefully
