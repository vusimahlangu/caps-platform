Chapter 1: Algebra

Equations, Inequalities & Nature of Roots (2026 CAPS)

1. Introduction

Algebra is the core of Paper 1. We solve for unknowns and determine the behaviour of quadratic graphs before drawing them.

2. Standard Rules & Formulae

The Quadratic Formula:

\[ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \]

Key Constraint: For roots to be Real, the discriminant must be \(\Delta \geq 0\).

3. Practical Examples

  1. Quadratic: Solve \(x^2 - 7x + 10 = 0\) by factorising.
  2. The Formula: Solve \(2x^2 + x - 5 = 0\) (Correct to 2 decimals).
  3. Inequality: Solve for \(x\): \(x^2 - 9 < 0\).
  4. Surds: Solve \(\sqrt{x + 5} = x - 1\). Check for invalid solutions!
