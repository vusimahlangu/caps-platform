Chapter 1: Algebra

Equations, Inequalities & Nature of Roots

1. Introduction

Algebra is the 25-mark anchor of Paper 1. We solve for unknowns and predict graphical behaviour using $\Delta$.

2. Rules & Formulae

The standard quadratic formula for $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ is:

\[ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \]

3. Five Core Examples

  1. Solve for $x$: $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$
  2. Use the formula: $3x^2 - 2x - 4 = 0$ (2 decimals)
  3. Inequality: $x^2 - 9 \geq 0$
  4. Simultaneous: $y + 2x = 2$ and $x^2 + y = 8$
  5. Surds: $\sqrt{x-1} + 3 = x$ (Test roots!)
